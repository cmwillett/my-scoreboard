Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

My Scoreboard is a personal sports scoreboard application for viewing sports scores, schedules, followed teams, favorite teams, golfers, and World Cup information.

Information we collect

My Scoreboard does not require user accounts, subscriptions, payment information, or personal profile information.

The app may store local preferences on your device, such as display density, selected page, or app display settings. These preferences are used only to improve your experience in the app.

Sports data

The app displays sports scores, schedules, statuses, TV information, and related sports data from third-party sports data sources and the owner's connected backend. This data is used only to power the scoreboard experience.

How information is used

  • To display live, upcoming, and final games.
  • To show selected teams, favorite teams, golfers, and World Cup information.
  • To remember local display preferences.
  • To support app functionality and troubleshooting.

Information sharing

My Scoreboard does not sell, rent, or share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

Third-party services

The app relies on sports data and hosting services to display scoreboard information. Those third-party services may have their own privacy practices.

Children's privacy

My Scoreboard is not designed to collect information from children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the My Scoreboard website or support link provided in the Roku channel listing.

This app is intended as a private/personal scoreboard and does not include advertising, payments, or account registration.
